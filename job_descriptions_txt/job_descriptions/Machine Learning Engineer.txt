Machine Learning Engineer

Job Description: The Machine Learning Engineer will be responsible for designing, developing, and implementing machine learning algorithms and models to solve business problems. They will work closely with data scientists and software engineers to develop and deploy scalable and efficient machine learning systems. The Machine Learning Engineer will also be responsible for maintaining and improving existing machine learning models, as well as conducting research to identify new machine learning technologies and techniques. Requirements: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, or related field. Master's or PhD preferred. 3+ years of experience in machine learning engineering. Strong programming skills in Python, Java, or C++. Experience with machine learning frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, or Keras. Experience with data processing and analysis tools such as Pandas, NumPy, or Scikit-Learn. Familiarity with cloud computing platforms such as AWS, Azure, or GCP. Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills. Strong communication and collaboration skills. Experience with Agile development methodologies preferred.
