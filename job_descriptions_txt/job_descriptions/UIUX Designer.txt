UI/UX Designer

Job Description: A UI/UX designer is responsible for creating intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces for websites and web applications. They use design tools such as Sketch or Adobe XD to create wireframes, prototypes, and user interface designs. Requirements: Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design or related field, at least 2 years of experience in UI/UX design, proficiency in design tools such as Sketch or Adobe XD, experience with user research and usability testing, strong problem-solving and analytical skills, excellent communication and collaboration skills.
