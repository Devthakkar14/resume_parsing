IT Project Manager

Job Description: An IT project manager is responsible for planning, executing, and monitoring IT projects from start to finish. They work closely with stakeholders to define project requirements, create project plans, and manage project budgets and timelines. Requirements: Bachelor's degree in Business Administration or related field, at least 5 years of experience in IT project management, experience with project management tools such as Jira or Trello, excellent organizational and time management skills, strong communication and collaboration skills.
