Software Engineer

Job Description: A software engineer is responsible for designing, developing, testing, and maintaining software applications. They use programming languages such as Java, Python, or C++ to create applications that meet customer needs. They also collaborate with other departments to ensure successful project completion. Requirements: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field, at least 3 years of experience in software development, proficiency in programming languages such as Java, Python, or C++, experience in software design and development methodologies, strong problem-solving and analytical skills, excellent communication and collaboration skills.
