Network Administrator

Job Description: A network administrator is responsible for managing and maintaining computer networks. They ensure that network systems are running smoothly and securely, troubleshoot network issues, and implement new network technologies. Requirements: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field, at least 2 years of experience in network administration, proficiency in network management tools such as Cisco or Juniper, experience in network security and firewalls, excellent problem-solving and analytical skills, strong communication and collaboration skills.
